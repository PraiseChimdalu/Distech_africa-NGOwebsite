DISTECH AFRICA
CHARITY FOUNDATION

DISTECH AFRICA is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering African communities through education, innovation,and sustainable development.

Who We are

At Distech Africa, we are a team of passionate individuals committed to transforming the lives of African youth through education, technology, and community development.

What we do

  • Empowering thousands of youth with digital skills
  • Enhancing health and wellness
  • Fostering economic growth and self-sufficiency
  • Improving access to quality education for communities
